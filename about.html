Our Story

We are just students who had depression and OCD and couldn't get help. Things got pretty ugly for both of us at certain points. So much so that we questioned life itself. But we got better. And to make sure that others don't have to go through what we did, we decided to create a platform where people could just share their feelings. We wanted to create a safe haven for everyone. So here we are!
