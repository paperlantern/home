I never lose, I win or I learn



Every morning of life brings some conditions, every evening of life gives some experiences. I think not more than 5% people are aware of the fact that what is the reason behind our Cantankerous behaviour. People love to consider this quality as a mandatory part of human nature but never tried to do research on that. Why? Because of virtual pool of hesitation and doubt on their will power.

Anything is possible. As long as you are healthy, alive and happy, you have the choice to keep trying until you finally succeed. But YES you have to be realistic. The words 'give' and 'up' should never be together. This is not a part of hierarchy even not a habit of your's but this is completely depend on our realization of belive on will power. Today is hard, tomorrow will be worse but the day after tomorrow will be sunshine. If you are feeling depressed you might need to make an important change in your life. In short, without failure there would be no heroic story- Keep going!!

-Kamakshi Gupta
